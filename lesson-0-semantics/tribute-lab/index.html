Ms.Lauryn Hill

The woman who changed a million lives

Here's a timeline of Ms.Lauryn Hill so far:
  • 1975 Born in South Orange, New Jersey

  • 1988 Performed "Who's Lovin You?" on Amateur Night at the Apollo

  • 1991 Fugees are formed

  • 1993 Co-starred in Sister Act 2

  • 1996 "The Score" debuted and earned The Fugees 2 Grammys

  • 1998 Began her solo career with the debut of the critically acclaimed album "The Miseducation of Lauryn Hill"
